## Benign Scenario Templates for False-Positive Testing

### Child - School & Study
- "Hey! Want to study for the math test together after school?"
- "Can you help me with the science project? We're in the same group."
- "The teacher said we need to submit the essay by Friday."
- "Did you finish the homework? I'm stuck on question 7."

### Child - Sports & Activities
- "Great game today! Your team played really well."
- "Are you coming to practice tomorrow? Coach said it's at 4pm."
- "I signed up for the coding club. You should join too!"
- "My mom is driving us to the tournament. Can you be ready by 8?"

### Child - Entertainment & Social
- "Did you see the new episode? It was so good!"
- "LOL that meme was so funny! Here's another one about cats."
- "I just beat the final boss! Took me 3 hours haha."
- "Are you going to the school dance? A bunch of us are going together."

### Child - Family & Events
- "My mom says I can come to your birthday party!"
- "We're having a sleepover this weekend. Ask your parents!"
- "My sister taught me how to make cookies. I'll bring some to school."
- "Happy birthday! Did you get the present I got you?"

### Senior - Family Check-ins
- "Hi Grandma! Just wanted to check in. How are you feeling today?"
- "Happy birthday! The family is planning a dinner at your favorite restaurant."
- "The kids want to video call you this weekend. Are you free Sunday?"
- "Mom, I picked up your prescription. I'll drop it off after work."

### Senior - Community & Social
- "The book club meets this Thursday at 2pm. See you there!"
- "The church potluck is this Sunday. I'm bringing my famous casserole!"
- "Your neighbor asked me to tell you the garden club meeting moved to Tuesday."
- "The senior center is offering free flu shots next week."

### Senior - Health & Appointments
- "Reminder: Your doctor's appointment is tomorrow at 10am."
- "Your prescription is ready for pickup at the pharmacy."
- "The physical therapy office confirmed your appointment for next Wednesday."
- "Your lab results came back normal. The doctor will call to discuss."

### Senior - Legitimate Services
- "Your grocery delivery is scheduled for 2pm today."
- "Your package from Amazon has been delivered to your front door."
- "Your electricity bill is due on the 15th. Current balance: $87.50."
- "Your library books are due back next Thursday."
